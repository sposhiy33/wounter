Worm Counting

Controls:

Point Edit Mode:

  • • Click to add/remove points
  • • Drag points to move them
  • • Ctrl+Z to undo

Select Mode:

  • • Click and drag for rectangular selection
  • • Click to toggle point selection
  • • Delete key to remove selected points
  • • Clear Selection button to deselect all

Zoom Controls:

  • • Ctrl + Plus/Minus or Ctrl + Scroll to zoom
  • • Ctrl + 0 to reset zoom
  • • Hold SPACE to hide/show points

Current mode: Point Edit - Click to add/remove points, drag to move them
